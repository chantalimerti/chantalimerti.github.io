Bats

I really love bats, I think theyre really interesting animals. There are around 1,300 different species of bats, thats 1/3 of our animal population! I usually keep this interest to myself cause people hate bats and think they're gross or weird. There's also the misconception that all bats are vampires, which isn't true; only three species are vampire bats and only two of those three specices mainly focus on mammalian blood. They also don't live on this continent. But yeah, they're beautiful creatures that help the environment a lot more than people think.
