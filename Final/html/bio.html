Other Interests

I've always loved to draw but only started taking it seriously in middle school. Most pieces are inspired by memories from my childhood, my dreams, and my thoughts. I want my art to sort of campture the imagination I had when I was younger, and also the innocence of being a kid. What I hated most about my childhood was that I grew up too fast, so most of my pieces are based on memories from pre-school to the 4th grade.
